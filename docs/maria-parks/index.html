Meet Maria Parks & What this is ALL About.

Who is this lady?

Hello! I am a special education teacher in Wisconsin. I have been blogging my journey in education on and off since I graduated from college (go UW-Whitewater!) in 2018. I wanted to take another, more serious stab, at it as the passion for what I am doing now, grows by the day.

How do myself and many other teachers do it?

We get really good at learning from other teachers in our building and online. We become masterful at finding free resources online, Teacher Pay Teachers becomes a go to resource, and we do not stop until we find what will help our students learn what they need to know.

So, I figure I can do a small part and share to the teaching community a bit about what I am doing in my classroom. Maybe someone out there will be able to use what I have created; which is mostly videos at this point (I am a self proclaimed You-Tuber since the pandemic and virtual learning hit).

Where my passion lies

Right now, my passion lies in life skills and pre-vocational skills. I think these are two of the MOST important skills for the students I work with. I am having a plethora of fun learning about these areas. You will see for now, my content is going to focus on these areas. It is something that makes me happy, and who doesn't need a bright spot in these WEIRD pandemic times we are in.
